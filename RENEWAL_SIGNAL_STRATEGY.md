# Renewal Signal Strategy

## Objective
Ensure sustained, visible, and traceable development activity throughout the 90-day cycle to support sandbox renewal.

## Signal Categories
- Daily UC Day documentation
- Microsoft Graph usage (read-only where applicable)
- Automation and telemetry readiness
- Forward planning for restored services

## Current Constraints
- SharePoint / OneDrive access denied
- Operating in read-only or planning mode where required

## Strategy
- Maintain daily cadence regardless of constraints
- Favor low-risk, high-visibility activities
- Escalate to high-impact execution immediately upon service restoration
